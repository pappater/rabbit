# Wandering Minstrel - Collection Outline

## Collection Structure

**Total Duration**: 300 days (approximately 10 months)
**Format**: One ballad poem per day
**Completion**: Series marks as complete after day 300

## Progression

### Phase 1: Days 1-100 - Foundation
Focus on establishing the collection with diverse ballad forms:
- Traditional folk ballads
- Literary ballads
- Story-driven narratives
- Character-focused tales
- Introduction to various ballad structures

### Phase 2: Days 101-200 - Exploration
Expand into varied themes and styles:
- Historical ballads
- Legendary and mythic narratives
- Romantic and tragic stories
- Heroic tales
- Experimental narrative forms

### Phase 3: Days 201-300 - Mastery
Culminate with sophisticated ballad compositions:
- Complex narrative structures
- Multi-layered storytelling
- Diverse cultural ballad traditions
- Contemporary narrative verse
- Synthesis of ballad elements

## Thematic Categories

The 300 poems will explore various ballad themes:

1. **Love and Romance** (~50 poems)
   - Romantic encounters
   - Tragic love stories
   - Courtship tales
   - Lost love

2. **Adventure and Quest** (~50 poems)
   - Hero's journeys
   - Quests and challenges
   - Travel narratives
   - Exploration stories

3. **Tragedy and Loss** (~50 poems)
   - Dramatic tragedies
   - Death and mourning
   - Betrayal tales
   - Fate and destiny

4. **Folklore and Legend** (~50 poems)
   - Mythical beings
   - Legendary heroes
   - Cultural folklore
   - Supernatural events

5. **Historical Events** (~40 poems)
   - Real historical moments
   - Famous figures
   - Social movements
   - War and peace

6. **Nature and Seasons** (~30 poems)
   - Natural phenomena
   - Seasonal changes
   - Animals and wilderness
   - Human-nature connection

7. **Social Commentary** (~30 poems)
   - Contemporary issues
   - Justice and injustice
   - Community stories
   - Human condition

## Poetry Forms Distribution

Variety of ballad and narrative forms across 300 poems:
- Traditional ballad form
- Border ballad
- Literary ballad
- Lyric ballad
- Dramatic ballad
- Narrative poem
- Story poem
- Ballad variations

## Completion Criteria

- Day 300: Final poem generated
- `completed` field set to `true` in chapters.json
- Workflow ceases automatic execution
- Collection remains available for reading
- No further poems added after completion

## Goals

- Honor ballad traditions while exploring new narratives
- Tell compelling stories through verse
- Maintain authentic ballad characteristics
- Create a cohesive yet diverse collection
- Complete the 300-poem journey
