# Wandering Minstrel

A daily ballad poetry collection exploring narrative verse forms, generated once daily for 300 days.

## About

"Wandering Minstrel" is a ballad poetry series that celebrates the rich tradition of narrative poetry. Each day, for 300 days, a new ballad poem is generated, drawing from various ballad forms and storytelling styles that have captivated audiences throughout literary history.

## Poetry Forms

The collection focuses on ballad forms and related narrative poetry, including:

- Traditional ballads: Folk ballad, Literary ballad, Border ballad
- Narrative forms: Story poems, Epic ballads, Heroic ballads
- Musical styles: Lyric ballads, Song ballads, Romantic ballads
- Modern adaptations: Contemporary ballads, Experimental narrative verse

## Generation Schedule

- **Frequency**: Once daily (14:00 UTC)
- **Duration**: 300 days (completes after 300 poems)
- **Format**: Individual ballad poems in narrative form
- **Style**: Authentic to ballad traditions and conventions
- **Length**: Varies by form (no artificial constraints)

## Features

- **Narrative Focus**: Each poem tells a story or explores a narrative theme
- **Authentic Forms**: Poems honor traditional ballad structures and techniques
- **Limited Series**: Exactly 300 poems, then marked as complete
- **Timestamped**: Each poem includes its publication date and time
- **Ballad Variety**: Different ballad styles and narrative approaches

## Technical Details

- **Generated by**: Google Gemini AI (gemini-2.5-flash)
- **Storage**: GitHub Gist
- **Automation**: GitHub Actions (daily workflow)
- **Configuration**: `scripts/wandering-minstrel/poetry_types.json`
