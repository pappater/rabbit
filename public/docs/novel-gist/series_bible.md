# Series Bible: The Weight of Promises

## Core Theme
Debt, mercy, and the burden of promises

## Setting
Late 19th century Russia, primarily in a provincial city and surrounding countryside. The story spans urban poverty, merchant households, and rural estates.

## Time Period
The narrative begins in autumn and will progress through the seasons, allowing for atmospheric changes that mirror the characters' internal transformations.

## Main Characters

### Dmitri Ivanovich Volkov
- Age: 34
- Background: Former military officer, now struggling with debts after his father's death
- Core conflict: Bound by a deathbed promise to care for his father's creditors and their families
- Personality: Honorable to a fault, tormented by conscience, prone to philosophical introspection
- Physical: Tall, dark-haired, prematurely aged by worry

### Anna Sergeyevna Orlova
- Age: 28
- Background: Widow of a merchant who owed Dmitri's father money; now living in genteel poverty
- Core conflict: Pride versus need; desire for independence versus debt to the Volkov family
- Personality: Intelligent, proud, compassionate, struggles with resentment and gratitude
- Physical: Delicate features, penetrating grey eyes, modest dress

### Pavel Fyodorovich Krasny
- Age: 45
- Background: Wealthy merchant, former business partner of Dmitri's father
- Core conflict: Greed versus guilt over unpaid debts and past betrayals
- Personality: Outwardly jovial, inwardly tortured, capable of both cruelty and unexpected kindness
- Physical: Portly, red-faced, expensive clothing

### Father Innokenty
- Age: 62
- Background: Village priest who serves as moral compass and confessor
- Role: Philosophical interlocutor, voice of mercy and forgiveness
- Personality: Wise, patient, sees the good in everyone, struggles with his own doubts
- Physical: White-bearded, gentle eyes, worn cassock

### Katerina (Katya) Volkov
- Age: 16
- Background: Dmitri's younger sister, sheltered and idealistic
- Core conflict: Coming of age in poverty, learning harsh truths about family history
- Personality: Innocent, romantic, gradually awakening to moral complexity
- Physical: Fair-haired, youthful beauty, expressive face

## Narrative Style
- Dostoevsky-influenced: psychological depth, moral philosophy, inner monologue
- Third-person omniscient with deep character interiority
- Rich sensory description balancing external action with internal struggle
- Extended dialogues that explore ideas and reveal character
- Symbolism: debt ledgers, seasons, religious imagery

## Tone
Serious, philosophical, emotionally intense, with moments of tenderness and occasional dark humor. The overall arc moves from despair toward hope, though not without continued struggle.
